Patch 2.55.16.96846 — April 20, 2026

General

  • New Mount: Scrapper Mad Axe Mono Wheel
  • Unranked Mode has been removed.
  • Respawn timers reduced in the mid-to-late game; by level 22, respawns are back to 65 seconds.
  • Updated home screen and startup music.

Map Updates

  • Garden of Terror Classic: Original map returns to Custom Games under this name (distinct from current Garden of Terror).
  • Haunted Mines: Returns to Quick Match. Mine Golem drops 10 extra Skulls over its life; objective completes at 55 Skulls (not 100). Hero vision is reduced in the mines.
  • Warhead Junction: Nuke cast time 2.5s → 3s; casting breaks on taking damage. Nuke DoT damage +100%. Nuke damage to Forts/Keeps 100% → 125%.

Hero Balance Changes

  • Arthas: Full rework — new Death Coil (damage or self-heal), Howling Blast, Frozen Tempest (stacking slows), charge-based Army of the Dead, Sindragosa heroic, and questing Frostmourne Hungers (D). Full talent tree replaced.
  • Chen: Keg Smash / Breath of Fire tuning (Breath initial damage down, duration up, no Energy cost; Keg Smash cost up). Fortifying Brew briefly blocks extra commands. Talent reshuffle: Accumulating Flames to 1 with reworked quest/Mythic, Eye of the Tiger to 4, Elusive Brawler move-speed nerf, Wandering Keg CD up, Pressure Point slow duration up.
  • Deathwing: Onslaught cooldown 5s → 6s; Dragonflight 15s → 20s. Gaze onto Destruction duration 5s → 3.5s.
  • Falstad: Frequent Flyer kill-credit range 14 → 8.5.
  • Garrosh: Wrecking Ball range and search range reduced. Unrivaled Strength ranged bonus 20% → 15%.
  • Gazlowe: Rock-It! Turret health up (510 → 600), decay over 20s (was 17). Robo-Goblin DPS 90 → 100.
  • Mal'Ganis: Necrotic Embrace — double Vampiric Touch benefit, 15 Spell Armor (was 25 Armor), damage up. Will of Tichondrius / Vampiric Aura / Fueled by Torment / Might of Sargeras level and effect changes.
  • Medivh: Winds of Celerity and Raven's Intellect buffed; Raven Familiar slow reduced; Force of Magic spell power up; Circle of Protection no longer increases Force of Will CD; Guardian of Tirisfal also +50% Arcane Rift range.
  • Muradin: Storm Bolt grants 5 stacks if the target dies within 3s of a hit; pierce quest threshold 40 → 45. Dwarf Launch CD reduction per hit 2s → 1s.
  • Sgt. Hammer: Maelstrom Rounds damage bonus 30% → 40%. Hyper-Cooling Engines / Ultra-Capacitors: CD reduction from Structures instead of splash. Advanced Lava Strike damage bonus 50% → 65%.
  • Thrall: Crash Lightning — fewer stacks lost on death. Echo of the Elements — longer kill window, higher stack thresholds, second reward no longer adds a bounce; other tuning. Rolling Thunder now grants an extra Chain Lightning bounce.
  • Tyrande: Lunar Flare — stack on kill within 3s, Mythic also grants +1 attack range, Hero-hit damage tuning. Quickening Blessing move bonus up. Commander of Sentinels no longer increases Sentinel cooldown (vs. PTR).

Bug Fixes

  • General: Unstoppable targets correctly immune to Blinds.
  • Maps: Bosses no longer prioritize attacking summoned units. Blackheart's Bay AI coin turn-in. Infernal Shrines minion pathing near Punisher beams.
  • Heroes: D.Va Defense Matrix AI retargeting; Dragonflight CD-on-damage rule; Garrosh In For the Kill; Gul'dan Drain Life interrupt; Jaina Improved Ice Block / AI; Kael'thas Rebirth AI; Li Li Cloud Serpent FoW visibility; Medivh Poly Bomb AI; Nazeebo Superstition Corpse Spider display; Samuro Mirror Image move/AI; Stukov Weighted Pustule vs. slow removal; Thrall Feral Spirit extension.
