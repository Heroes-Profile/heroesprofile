Patch 2.55.15.96443 — February 19, 2026 (Balance Patch)

Balance Updates

  • Deathwing: Incinerate cooldown increased from 6s to 7s. Death Drop Spell Armor duration reduced from 6s to 4s.
  • Muradin: Storm Bolt quest requirements reduced (50/200 → 40/160). Give 'Em The Axe bonus damage reduced from 50% to 40%. Sledgehammer now also reduces Storm Bolt cooldown by 1.5s. Perfect Storm now restores 25 Mana on reset, internal cooldown reduced. Healing Static heal per Hero hit increased from 5% to 6%.
  • Murky: Revive Shield duration increased from 10s to 15s. March of the Murlocs cooldown reduced from 110s to 100s. Egg Shell shield now lasts until destroyed and increased from 35% to 50%. Never-Ending March now lasts a minimum of 7 seconds.
  • Sylvanas: Lost Soul extra 10% damage bonus removed.
  • Tyrande: Lunar Flare Mythic reward requirements reduced from 45 to 40 Hero hits.

Bug Fixes

  • Maps: Hanamura Samurai Sentinel camp now shows Bruiser icon. Braxis Holdout terrain rendering fix.
  • Azmodan: Battleborn now properly reduces Demon Lieutenant cooldown. Black Pool now deals reduced damage to non-Heroes.
  • Deathwing: AI can no longer use the removed Form Swap ability.
  • Gul'dan: Ruinous Affliction now correctly triggers on 3rd and 6th hit.
  • Jaina: Ice Cold no longer allows moving through pathing blockers. Ice Block tooltip now shows correct cooldown.
  • Muradin: Dwarf Toss no longer goes on cooldown on invalid terrain. Dwarf Block can no longer stack beyond 3 charges.
  • Murky: Fixed losing increased size after death. Fixed respawning faster than intended. Shell Game and Faster Fish UI fixes.
  • Rehgar: Elemental Conduit shield refresh now correctly searches around Rehgar and properly refreshes to 5 seconds.
  • Thrall: Echo of the Elements no longer bounces to cloaked targets. Rolling Thunder and Alpha Wolf damage now display as crits.
  • Zagara: Roaches now display correctly in death recap and use melee attacks instead of missiles.
