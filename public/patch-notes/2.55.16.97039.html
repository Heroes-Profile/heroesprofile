Patch 2.55.16.97039 — May 11, 2026 (Balance Patch)

Map Updates

  • Haunted Mines: Skull scaling increased (5% → 7%). Golem scaling reduced early (before 5 minutes) and increased significantly in the late game.

Hero Balance Changes

  • Arthas: Base health and Death Coil healing increased. Frostmourne Hungers — lower damage per quest stack on Basic Attacks, higher damage bonus on charged attacks. Frost Presence: line damage can hit in both the line and the final area; Death Coil slow from the talent lasts longer. Icebound Fortitude clears Slows and Roots on use. Path of Frost sets minimum Movement Speed to 140% instead of flat +40% Movement Speed.
  • Chen: Flying Kick damage up. Fortifying Brew — slightly faster interrupt window, more shield per tick. Accumulating Flame: initial hit grants a quest stack; higher stack thresholds (25/80).
  • Deathwing: Molten Flame damage per tick increased. Destroyer's Rampage duration reduced (6s → 4s).
  • Gazlowe: Rock-It! Turret damage up. One Man Wrecking Crew damage bonus increased (60% → 75%).
  • Mal'Ganis: Will of Tichondrius — lower damage per stack, easier quest unlock. Winged Guard stacks slightly more often. Deep Sleep move-speed bonus up. Wrath of Nathreza transfers more damage to Mal'Ganis.
  • Murky: March of the Murlocs cooldown reduced (100s → 90s).

Bug Fixes

  • Maps: Volskaya Foundry — Triglav Protector Particle Cannon charge no longer causes other players' cursors to highlight incorrectly.
  • Arthas: Rime and Remorseless Winter healing now display correctly in Death Recap.
  • Chen: Breath of Fire again deals impact damage.
  • Muradin: Storm Bolt / Perfect Storm quest UI, Mana refund, cooldown, and Self Healing score fixes.
  • Rexxar: Misha respawns correctly when her respawn timer finishes.
  • Tychus / Zagara: Infest displays as a Quest Bonus where applicable.
  • Tyrande: Lunar Blaze quest progress fixes (non-standard death states, non-Hero kills, progression indicator).
