Patch 2.55.17.97605 — July 20, 2026 (Live Patch)

General Updates

  • New skin: Ruby Chromesthesia Lucio.
  • Haunted Mines added to Ranked play.
  • Players can now party with unplaced players in Storm League.
  • Updated homescreen and startup music.

Map Updates

  • Hanamura Temple: Additional brush added in several locations.
  • Warhead Junction: Warhead channel penalty on interrupt reduced (10s → 5s).

Hero Balance Changes

  • Abathur: Locust Strain reworked — health 350 → 225, damage 58 → 36, Locusts now self-heal (20% of damage dealt, 20% max health when a minion dies nearby), bonus damage to non-Heroes now 100%. Envenomed Nests armor reduction 10 → 15. Locust Brood moved to Level 1 (cooldown 45s → 75s); Survival Instincts moved to Level 16. New Level 4 talent Slapathur: Basic Attacks deal 350% bonus damage and stun for 3 seconds. Evolve Monstrosity now displays as a Quest with a unit frame.
  • Alexstrasza: Dragonqueen cooldown 150s → 130s (now starts after expiration). Breath of Life healing 20% → 15%; Preservation healing 30% → 25%. Flames of Fury cooldown 5s → 6s with a new Mythic quest (hit 40 Heroes without dying to refresh Dragonqueen duration). Lifeblossom removes Slows/Roots and persists through transformation. Draconic Discipline now reduces Preservation cooldown per Hero healed instead of extending duration.
  • Blaze: Adrenaline Stimpack Attack Speed bonus 100% → 85%. Bunker Drop cooldown 80s → 85s.
  • Cho: Consuming Fire healing bonus becomes flat 75 health; Calloused Hide threshold 60% → 50%. Firestarter cooldown reduction 1s → 1.5s per attack.
  • Gall: Rising Dread bonus 25% → 20%. Bomb's Away stacks uncapped but damage 5 → 4 per stack. Double Trouble cooldown reduction 0.5s → 1s. Leaden Orb stun 0.75s → 0.6s.
  • Chromie: Dragon's Breath now shows a radius preview to enemies. Time Trap health 400 → 250. Once Again The First Time quest 40 → 20 stacks but requires no deaths. Deep Breathing 1% → 0.5% per stack, uncapped. Here and There range 20 → 40 with snap targeting.
  • Diablo: Shadow Charge damage 40 → 45; Overpower damage 73 → 80; Fire Stomp damage reduced (12 → 11, return 36 → 34). Diabolical Momentum cooldown reduction 1.5s → 2s per attack.
  • Genji: Swift as the Wind Movement Speed 30% → 40%; Pathfinder duration 4s → 6s; Perfect Defense and Flow Like Water cooldown reduction improved. Final Cut damage 125 → 115.
  • Hogger: On the Prowl rage generation 30 → 25. Hogger's Joggers rage threshold 30 → 35.
  • Johanna: Shield Glare cooldown 12s → 13s. Blessed Shield cooldown 60s → 65s.
  • Mal'Ganis: Will of Tichondrius: the talent's bonus stun duration reduced from 0.5s to 0.25s. Blind as a Bat now has a 0.2s reactivation delay between swipes.
  • Qhira: Blood Rage gains a baseline quest (+0.25% damage per stack); bleed damage 11 → 8. Revolving Sweep can no longer be detached by the target becoming Unstoppable. Fatal Wounds bonus 30% → 50% against low-health Heroes (quest removed).
  • Ragnaros: Molten Core reworked — cooldown 120s → 180s, health 3996 → 5000, duration 18s → 23s, now stuns for 1 second on exit; ability range starts at 20 and grows to 35 over 9 seconds. Molten Swing damage 161 → 250 with knockback (cooldown 6s → 9s). Meteor Shower damage 151 → 200, +66% area, 35% slow (cooldown 2.5s → 6s). Explosive Rune cooldown 4s → 2.5s, damage now 440 over 5 seconds. Blast Wave damage 104 → 120. Sulfuras Smash gains a new Mythic quest (hit 8 Heroes for an extra charge).
  • Thrall: Crash Lightning disabled in ARAM.
  • Whitemane: Searing Lash gains a new quest (+3 max health, +2 mana per Hero hit). Inquisitor's Prayer restores 25 mana per Hero and extends Zeal by 2 seconds. Intercession cooldown 45s → 60s. Scarlet Wrath mana return 0.5% → 1%.
  • Yrel: Basic ability buffs with mana costs reduced (65 → 50): Vindication heal 320 → 370, Righteous Hammer stun 0.75s → 1s, Avenging Wrath slow 50% → 60%. Divine Purpose cooldown 8s → 12s; basic abilities now reduce it by 3 seconds on interrupt and it no longer resets on heroic use.

Bug Fixes

  • General: Fort, Keep, and Core attack beams are now more visible when targeting Heroes.
  • Maps: Haunted Mines — fixed auto-mount issue when using Hearthstone inside the mines. Warhead Junction — fixed Hero visibility during Warhead launch and persistent channel beam on interrupt.
  • Ana: Eye of Horus no longer reveals global lines at structures; minor damage delay removed.
  • Arthas: Howling Blast visuals now match its area; Froststrike slow baseline issues resolved.
  • Deathwing: No longer teleports if killed during Cataclysm.
  • D.Va: Defense Matrix damage reduction now properly applies to summoned units.
  • Jaina: Ultimate Evolution clones now correctly inherit spell power across multiple abilities.
  • Nazeebo: Voodoo Ritual quest indicator and tooltip corrections.
  • Xul: Skeletal unit Defense Matrix and evasion interaction fixes.
  • Yrel: Avenging Wrath is no longer interrupted by Roots during channeling.
  • Additional fixes for Anub'arak, Deckard, Hogger, Kerrigan, Leoric, Malfurion, Medivh, Nova, Probius, Tychus, and Zagara.
